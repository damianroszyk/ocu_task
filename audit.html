Audit

A "mobile first" approach to FFA.
Fully offline operational. Highly ergonomic.

Project Summary

The inFullMobile team was challanged with creating a global FFA solution for one of our clients. One that would further optimise existing processes and eventualy bringing significant efficiency savings for the company.

Project Scope
FFA iOS application + Integration with existing backend

Deliverables
Design, Development, Management, QA

Project Details

Application is used by the store shelves auditors while checking the SOS and OOS metrics. Auditors visiting retailer locations in many cases are interacting with managers or points of contact from retailer side. Audit app is a one stop shop for task management, surveying, calendar and master data management.

The Solution

inFullMobile team from the very beginning follwed a user-centric approach. Walking in the shoes of the field representative gave our designers and developers great perspective and triggered lof of UI adjustments. Those adjustments converted into efficiency savings after the project delivery.

application design guidelines

Application Design / UX guidlines assure simple and fast mobile experiences to the user, similar to those they are already having on their mobile devices. Take a look at some of major outcomes below.
