Project Details

PLEXICON is a very demanding project. It combines physical goods and software in order to create a stunning and memorable experience. It is the perfect outlet for the appreciation of mobile business. We learned how to build a strong brand from scratch and fuse two very different worlds together. PLEXICON lets you print your best Instagram™ shots using finest quality photo frames that will stay with you forever.

Our Solution

We knew that app as simple as Instagram™ needed an equivalently frictionless service on our side. PLEXICON is minimal at its core and we took extra care to deliver the easiest way of ordering your best shots.

INDUSTRIAL design

The nature of the photos dictated the form of our photo frame design. Square sheets of crystal-clear acrylic glass are supported by a circular hanger. PLEXICONs can be hung at any desired angle or be rested on table tops using a stand.
